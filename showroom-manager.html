Sales Executive Availability· Live workload across the network
info Status logic — what each badge means and when it triggers — click to expand
On Track Green
Executive is available to receive new leads and is within operating limits.
Availability = Available · active leads below capacity · pending first contact within SLA · overdue commitments low · no waiting customer beyond threshold · no unconfirmed TD within 30 min · no repeated SLA breach today.
Due Soon Amber
Executive has a time-sensitive task but is not yet in breach.
First-contact SLA due in 10–15 min · appointment/TD starts in 30 min unconfirmed · callback due soon · temporarily unavailable but expected back.
Blocked Blue / Red
Executive should not receive more leads until a blocker is resolved.
Customer waiting without accepted handover · pending FC near breach · TD requested but slot not confirmed · unresolved overdue commitments · lead stuck with this exec · language mismatch.
Stalled Grey
Executive has dormant active opportunities that have not progressed.
Active opportunity no movement >7 days · quote not followed up · no next action · missed & not rescheduled commitment. Not the same as Lost.
At Capacity Grey-blue
Executive is available but should not receive more new leads.
Active leads exceed capacity · appointments today over threshold · TDs today over threshold · pending FC + overdue too high · with customer.
Offline Grey
Executive is not available (replaces 'Lost' — Lost is a lead status, not an exec status).
Not logged in · off shift · on break · absent · manually marked unavailable.
